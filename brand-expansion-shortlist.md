## Delegate brief — run per brand, gate first
1. **Margin gate (pass/fail):** pull live Amazon street price on the main styles → does (street price − our loaded cost) leave acceptable margin? **Fail = drop, stop here.**
2. **Survivors — search demand:** is the brand / its hero styles genuinely searched?
3. **Channel falls out** of (1)+(2): strong search + crowded/grey Amazon → Shopify-core; search + winnable Amazon → Amazon-core; both → run both, lead with stronger; weak search → drop.

**Output per brand:** the 2–5 hero styles that pass the gate and score on search, with the channel call. Work Priority 1 first.
